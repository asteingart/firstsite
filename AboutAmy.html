About Amy:

Born and raised near Boston, Massachusetts, Amy was fortunate enough to begin traveling with her family at a young age. In college, she had the opportunity to live abroad for a year in Tübingen, Germany and has always loved learning about other countries, cultures and languages. Amy now works at a nonprofit that focuses on creating mentoring relationships for girls in Boston, but she still makes travel a priority whenever she can! This website showcases some of Amy's more recent trips, highlighting her favorite places and offering tips for seeing the world on a budget.
